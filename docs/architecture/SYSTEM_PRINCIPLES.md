# AEGIS System Principles

Author: Ken Tannenbaum Project: AEGIS Version: 0.1

## Overview

The AEGIS architecture is guided by several foundational principles that
define how intelligent capability must be controlled.

## Principle 1 --- Bounded Capability

No intelligent system may exercise capability beyond explicitly
authorized scope.

## Principle 2 --- Complete Mediation

All capability requests must pass through governance evaluation before
execution.

## Principle 3 --- Auditability

Every decision must produce an auditable record.

## Principle 4 --- Deterministic Governance

Policy definitions must produce reproducible outcomes.

## Principle 5 --- Explicit Authority

Authority boundaries must be defined and enforced by the governance
system.

## Principle 6 --- Governance First

System capability is subordinate to governance decisions.
