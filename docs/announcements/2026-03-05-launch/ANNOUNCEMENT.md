## Announcement

Today we are publicly releasing **AEGIS™**, an open governance architecture designed to bring deterministic control, accountability, and policy enforcement to intelligent systems.

As AI systems become more capable and autonomous, the industry faces a growing challenge: **how to ensure intelligent systems act within defined boundaries before they interact with real infrastructure.**

AEGIS addresses this problem by introducing a governance layer that sits between intelligent systems and the environments in which they operate.

Rather than relying solely on training or post-hoc monitoring, AEGIS enforces **structured governance at the architectural level**.

---

## What AEGIS Provides

AEGIS introduces a framework for governing AI behavior through explicit policy and authorization mechanisms.

Key properties include:

* **Deterministic governance** of AI actions before execution
* **Capability-based authorization** for fine-grained control
* **Policy-driven enforcement** of system rules
* **Risk-based decision making** to evaluate proposed actions
* **Federated governance networks** for distributed control
* **Comprehensive audit trails** for accountability and review

Together, these components allow organizations to build systems where **intelligence operates within explicit, enforceable constraints**.
