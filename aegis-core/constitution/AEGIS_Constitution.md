# AEGIS Constitution

Version 0.1

## Article I — Bounded Capability
No AI system may access a capability not explicitly defined in the capability registry.

## Article II — Authority Verification
All actions must be attributable to a verifiable actor.

## Article III — Deterministic Enforcement
Governance must be enforced by system architecture.

## Article IV — Operational Safety
Destructive actions require explicit human authorization.

## Article V — Data Protection
Sensitive data must not be exposed without authorization.

## Article VI — Auditability
All system actions must produce immutable audit records.

## Foundational Maxim
Capability without constraint is not intelligence.
